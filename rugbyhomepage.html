The Gamecocks take home the 2023 SCRC Championship!!

The Gamecock Rugby Team is a Division 1 Rugby Club in the Southeastern Rugby Conference. This group of young men are are of all ages growing as men both on and off the pitch . With the leadership of John Roberts, the Gamecocks have won two SCRC championships. The Gamecocks are en-route to Nationals this April and strive to make a large impact on the national stage in the premier division.

This club is nothing without our supporters! We urge everyone to make a donation big or small to continue this track of success!
