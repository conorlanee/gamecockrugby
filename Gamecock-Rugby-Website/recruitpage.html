John Roberts graduate class of 1990 of the University of South Carolina. Earning a Masters degree in Higher Education at Columbia College. Worked at Furman University for 22 and served as their head rugby coach. Compiled a career record of 188-64 and guided the Paladins to three D2 National Championships.

Jeff Smolka a University of South Carolina graduate of 1989 remained connected to the program for over three decades. Served as a headocach and proud member of the Carolina Rugby Foundation. For many years, Jeff managed the club blood drive and helped to launch the Carolina Rugby Endowment by establishing the Jeffery S. Smolka Scholarship in 2016.
